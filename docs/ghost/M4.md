# Ghost M4 — No Pro Tier

**Phase:** N/A (Phase 3 does not apply to Ghost)
**Status:** Decision made — no work planned
**Depends on:** M3

---

## Decision

**Ghost has no pro tier. Ghost stays free and minimal.**

This is a deliberate product decision documented in the master roadmap. Ghost's identity as a privacy-first, zero-footprint browser is incompatible with the "pro tier" model. There is no feature in Ghost's scope that strains the minimalist ethos in the way that justifies a paid upsell — privacy is not a premium feature, it is the product.

Ghost completes its roadmap at M3 (security audit & hardening). After M3, Ghost is done. No additional milestones are planned.

---

## What "Done" Means for Ghost

After M3 ships:
- Session isolation, cookie clearing, tracker blocking are implemented and tested
- Settings UI covers all user-configurable options
- Browser fundamentals (zoom, fullscreen, find-in-page) work correctly
- Full security audit has passed (CSP, toolbar XSS vectors, WebRTC leak prevention)
- Privacy features are verified by automated tests (no data persists after close)

Ghost at M3 is a complete, shippable product.

---

## Future Work (If Any)

If Ghost ever gains features that justify a new milestone, they should be added as a new M4 with a concrete scope — not retrofitted into this document. The absence of M4 scope today is intentional, not an oversight.
